Welcome to Emit's documentation!
================================

Emit is a library that hooks into Celery (or just local memory) to provide
subscriptions and notifications for your functions. It makes stream processing a
whole lot easier.

Supported Pythons:

* CPython 2.7.2
* CPython 3.2
* PyPy 1.9
